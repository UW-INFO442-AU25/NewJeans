# User Personas

## 1) International Student – "Anika" (F-1, STEM)
- Goals: Find visa-friendly internships; understand CPT/OPT timelines; track document status.
- Behaviors: Checks jobs weekly, uses mobile, saves roles, uploads passport/I-20.
- Pain Points: Confusing timelines; slow document reviews; uncertainty on H-1B path.
- Success Criteria: Clear next steps, fast uploads, accurate visa filters, progress tracker.

## 2) New Grad – "Miguel" (OPT -> H-1B)
- Goals: Land full-time with sponsorship; know H-1B filing windows; share docs with employer.
- Behaviors: Desktop-first; compares companies; follows checklists; expects status sync.
- Pain Points: Mixed info across sites; worries about missing lottery dates; wants verified doc status.
- Success Criteria: Interactive checklists; reminders for deadlines; verified/green status on uploads.

## 3) Employer HR – "Samantha" (Tech recruiter)
- Goals: Source candidates who are work-eligible; understand CPT/OPT/H-1B constraints; validate documents.
- Behaviors: Uses desktop; scans job board filters; downloads candidate docs; checks sponsorship readiness.
- Pain Points: Unclear work authorization; slow access to documents; needs compliance clarity.
- Success Criteria: Trustworthy visa filters; quick doc retrieval; concise sponsorship guidance.

## 4) Career Services Advisor – "Leo"
- Goals: Guide students on compliant employment; review checklists; ensure deadlines are met.
- Behaviors: Reviews student profiles; checks checklist completion; recommends resources.
- Pain Points: Students forget steps; fragmented guidance; lacks real-time status.
- Success Criteria: Centralized checklists; visible completion; printable/exportable guidance.
